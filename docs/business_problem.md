# Business Problem Statement

## Overview

A boutique investment firm requires a real-time portfolio risk monitoring solution to better understand market exposure and portfolio performance.

## Challenges

* Limited visibility into portfolio risk.
* Difficulty identifying highly correlated assets.
* Inability to forecast potential portfolio losses.
* Lack of dynamic risk monitoring capabilities.

## Proposed Solution

Develop a Portfolio Risk Monitoring Dashboard that provides:

* Portfolio performance tracking
* Value at Risk (VaR) analysis
* Correlation Heatmaps
* Rolling Volatility metrics
* Monte Carlo Simulation forecasts

## Business Benefits

* Improved risk management
* Better diversification decisions
* Faster investment analysis
* Data-driven portfolio monitoring
