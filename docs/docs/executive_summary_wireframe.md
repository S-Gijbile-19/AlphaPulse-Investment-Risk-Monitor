# Executive Summary Dashboard Wireframe

## Dashboard Layout

---

## | Executive Summary                              |

## | Current VaR | Max Drawdown | Risk Status       |

## | Portfolio Overview                             |

## | Portfolio Risk Trend                           |

## | Key Insights and Recommendations               |

## KPI Section

### Current VaR

Displays estimated portfolio loss under normal market conditions.

### Max Drawdown

Displays the largest portfolio decline observed.

### Risk Status

Shows portfolio risk category.

## Benefits

* Quick decision-making
* Clear visibility of risk exposure
* Easy interpretation for executives
